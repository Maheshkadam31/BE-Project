About Us

Intro

Theatre by definition is for an audience; the purpose is to co-exist in a space shared between maker and audience. We try our best to give them a remarkable experience while and after watching our plays. We equally put in our efforts into developing characters, relatable stories, language, music, and spectacle. As these elements have formed the backbone of our successful plays.

Our Planning

We are trying to spread our reach and achieve a stage where we can conduct shows, participate in various state and national level competitions, perform at well-known theatre festivals, and also to easily give a chance to many performers and artists to showcase their passion for art.
